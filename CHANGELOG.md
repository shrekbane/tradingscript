- `feat(grading)`: lower the ADX "strong trend" hard-gate threshold from 20 to 15, to increase how often setups qualify without adding a new confluence factor. At 20, `adxStrong` was blocking moderately-trending markets (ADX 15-20) outright regardless of how strongly every other factor scored â€” 15 still screens out genuinely flat/choppy conditions, just with more room. Introduced `adxThreshold = 15` as a plain variable (not exposed as an input, per the chosen option) and anchored both `adxStrong = adxVal > adxThreshold` and the ADX scoring formula (`(adxVal - adxThreshold) / 20 * 2`, previously hardcoded to the literal `20` in three separate places) to it, so the scored contribution always starts from 0 exactly at the hard-gate boundary and can never go negative â€” this was a real risk if the gate's threshold moved without also moving the scoring anchor, since the scoring formula would otherwise start below zero for ADX values between the old and new threshold. Full-strength ADX for scoring purposes moved from 40 to 35 as a result (same 20-point-wide scaling range, just shifted down with the new anchor). Documented in `README.md` (factor list, "always-on requirements" paragraph, and a new note on the threshold change) and `index.html` (factor grid and "always on, not scored" callout).
